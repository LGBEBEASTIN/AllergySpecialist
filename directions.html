Directions

If you are coming to our Stockbridge Clinic, using I-75 South:
- Take Exit 224
- Turn Left
- Go across the interstate
- At the 1st Traffic Light, Turn Left on Rock Quarry Road (At the traffic light, there is a Texaco Gas Station on your Right)
- Go through 1 Traffic Light
- Building # 1520 will be on your Right (Across from Mexican Restaurant)
- We are located on the Upper Floor.

If you are coming to our Stockbridge Clinic, using I-75 North: - Take Exit 224
- Turn Right
- At the 1st Traffic Light, Turn Left on Rock Quarry Road (At the traffic light, there is a Texaco Gas Station on your Right)
- Go through 1 Traffic Light
- Building # 1520 will be on your Right (Across from Mexican Restaurant)
